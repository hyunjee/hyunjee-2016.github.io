Mexican Children's Day comes early with Día del Niño Family Festival

The 19th Annual Día del Niño Health Walk and Family Festival celebrated the importance of children and a healthy lifestyle Saturday, April 11, 2015. It was an early celebration of Mexican Children's Day which is April 30. Hosted by the National Museum of Mexican Art, the event began with a 2.1 mile health walk from the museum to the University of Illinois at Chicago Forum. The sun and high-50s temperatures greeted the families. Once at the Forum, the children had the opportunity to get creative with arts and crafts and get active with interactive games.
A photojournalism story I did for my multimedia journalism class freshman year.
