How to Ride a Bicycle

Now that the snow and ice have melted, the roads are safe for bicyclists again. Sasha Costello, a Medill freshman who has been biking for 10 years now, shares her tips on how to ride a bicycle. From checking that the area is clear and safe to stepping on the pedal, she explains it all.
