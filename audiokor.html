Loyolacappella seniors sing goodbye

Loyolacappella, Loyola University Chicago's premiere co-ed a cappella group, showcased nine songs at its end-of-the-year Lake Show April 27, 2015 in the university's McCormick Lounge. Almost 100 people attended the event. There were two senior members, Cole Johnson and Grace Affeltranger, who were performing for the last time as part of Loyolacappella. Both soon-to-be graduates plan to continue producing and performing music.
